Water Recipe Calculator
Two-concentrate method (Epsom salt + baking soda)
▾
Make the stocks once: GH stock = 24.65 g Epsom salt (MgSO₄·7H₂O) topped to 1 L with distilled water · KH stock = 16.8 g baking soda (NaHCO₃) topped to 1 L. Each stock ≈ 10,000 ppm as CaCO₃. SCA targets: GH 50–175, KH ≈ 40.
